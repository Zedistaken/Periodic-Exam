Ideal Weight Calculator

The Ideal Wight Calculator is an online program that computes the ideal bodyweight (IBW) of a person, that ranges based on height,and gender. It uses different formulas specifically, the Robinson, Miller, Hamwi, and Devine Formula which will compute depending on what height,weight, gender you have.
